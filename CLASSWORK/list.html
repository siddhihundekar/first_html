Vadapao Recipe

More about vadapao:

Vada Pav is also known as theIndian Burger. It is the one of the tastiest fast food of Maharashtra and also a famous chaat in Mumbai city.

Ingredients:
    For Vada :-
    • Potato
    • Garlic
    • Green chilli
    • Curry leaves
    • Mustard seeds
    • Turmeric powder
    • Cilantro
    • Salt
    • Oil
    For Coating :-
    • Besan
    • Water
    • Salt

Procedure:
  1. Cook the potatoes and mash them well.
  2. Take a pan and heat oil.
    1. Add some mustard seeds and let them splutter.
    2. Now add the curry leaves and crushed garlic pods and the green chillies.
    3. Add a little turmeric powder and stir well.
    4. Now add the mashed potatoes and salt. Mix well.
    5. Add some cilantro leaves and lemon juice. Mix well and keep aside. Let the mixture cool.
  3. Take the besan and add some water and salt (to taste). Make a batter. Do not make it very watery. (The batter should be a bit thick so that it forms a coating on the vada.)
  4. Heat 2 cups oil in a frying pan.
  5. Now take the potato mixture and make small balls out of it.
  6. Dip this ball into the besan batter and put them in the oil.
  7. Fry till done.
Some call it a snack, some say it's a little break food, and a hunger call it a 'VADAPAV' of 'MUMBAIKAR'
